The Big 6 Leaders

NVIDIA

Hardware & Computing

The absolute king of AI hardware. Their H100 and Blackwell GPUs power nearly every major AI model in existence, including ChatGPT.

TESLA

Robotics & Autonomy

Moving AI into the physical world. Tesla uses massive neural networks for Full Self-Driving (FSD) cars and the Optimus humanoid robot.

OPENAI

Generative AI

The creators of ChatGPT and Sora. They are leading the race towards AGI (Artificial General Intelligence) with powerful reasoning models.

GOOGLE

Data & Research

With DeepMind and the Gemini models, Google is integrating AI into search, phones, and scientific discovery.

MICROSOFT

Software Integration

Through Copilot, Microsoft is embedding AI into every PC and office software, making AI a daily productivity tool.

META

Open Source

Meta is democratizing AI by releasing their Llama models for free, allowing developers everywhere to build their own AI applications.
