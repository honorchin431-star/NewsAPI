The Big Players

These are the 6 companies currently defining the Artificial Intelligence landscape.

N

Nvidia

The Engine

Nvidia builds the GPUs (Chips) that power almost every AI system on Earth. Without them, modern AI simply wouldn't exist.

T

Tesla

Real World AI

Tesla focuses on applying AI to the physical world through Self-Driving Cars (FSD) and Humanoid Robots (Optimus).

O

OpenAI

The Pioneer

Creators of ChatGPT and Sora. They kicked off the current AI boom and lead the world in Large Language Model (LLM) research.

G

Google / DeepMind

The Researcher

With their Gemini models, Google is integrating powerful AI into Search, Android, and Workspace to make information universally accessible.

M

Microsoft

The Integrator

Through Copilot, Microsoft puts AI into every PC and Office app. They are also the biggest investor in OpenAI.

∞

Meta (Facebook)

Open Source Leader

Unlike others, Meta releases their powerful Llama AI models for free, allowing developers everywhere to build their own AI tools.
