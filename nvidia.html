NVIDIA AI

Powering the Intelligence Era

Nvidia is not just a chip company anymore. It is the backbone of the entire Artificial Intelligence revolution.

What They Do

NVIDIA designs the most powerful GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) in the world. Originally made for video games, these chips turned out to be perfect for training AI.

Their "CUDA" software allows developers to talk to these chips easily. Today, companies like OpenAI, Google, and Tesla all rely on NVIDIA's H100 and Blackwell chips to train their massive AI models.
